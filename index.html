Our Signature Treatment

Honey Massage

Immerse yourself in the healing properties of pure honey, meticulously applied using traditional Mongolian massage techniques. This step detoxifies the skin, promotes circulation, and leaves you feeling rejuvenated and radiant.

Hot Towel Wipe Down

Experience the soothing warmth of our hot towel wipe down, which removes impurities and enhances blood flow. This gentle process prepares your body for deeper relaxation and maximizes the benefits of the honey massage.

Olive or Coconut Oil Massage

Conclude your treatment with a nourishing virgin olive or coconut oil massage. This final step hydrates your skin, alleviates muscle tension, and induces a state of profound relaxation, ensuring a complete and revitalizing experience. *Coconut oil is available upon request as an alternative to olive oil.*

Honey for Stress Relief

Honey is rich in antioxidants and has natural anti-inflammatory properties. When used in massages, it helps soothe the nervous system, reduce cortisol levels, and promote relaxation, effectively combating daily stress.

Olive Oil for Muscle Recovery

Olive oil is packed with healthy fats and vitamins E and K, which nourish the skin and muscles. Its moisturizing properties help reduce muscle soreness and improve flexibility, making it ideal for recovery after a long day or intense physical activity.

Coconut Oil for Deep Hydration

Coconut oil is renowned for its deep moisturizing capabilities. It penetrates the skin to provide lasting hydration, leaving your skin soft, smooth, and revitalized. Additionally, its antibacterial properties help maintain healthy skin.

Combined Honey, Olive, and Coconut Oil Benefits

The combination of honey, olive oil, and coconut oil in massages enhances their individual benefits, providing deep hydration, improved circulation, and a calming effect on both the body and mind. This synergy is perfect for alleviating stress and promoting overall well-being.
